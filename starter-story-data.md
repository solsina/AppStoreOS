# Starter Story - Business Case Studies Database

This document contains a curated list of successful solo-founder and small-team business case studies from Starter Story. Each entry includes details on the founder, revenue, tech stack, and strategic analysis of why the business succeeded.

---

## 1. Carrd
- **Founder:** AJ
- **Revenue:** $1M+ ARR
- **Category:** Website Builder
- **Description:** Simple, fast, and fully responsive platform for creating one-page websites for almost anything.
- **Why It Works:** Extremely easy to use, very low pricing (generous freemium, then $19/year), perfect for creators, portfolios, and quick landing pages. The founder remained solo for a long time by automating everything.
- **Tech Stack:** JavaScript, AWS, Stripe
- **Source:** Starter Story Interview

---

## 2. Photopea
- **Founder:** Ivan Kutskir
- **Revenue:** $1M+ ARR
- **Category:** Design Tool
- **Description:** Advanced web-based image editor, compatible with PSD (Photoshop), Sketch, and XCF files. Essentially a free Photoshop clone in the browser.
- **Why It Works:** Solves a massive pain point (editing PSDs without paying for an Adobe subscription). Monetization via ads (very profitable due to massive traffic) and a premium ad-free version. A single developer maintains the entire project.
- **Tech Stack:** JavaScript, WebGL, HTML5 Canvas
- **Source:** Starter Story Interview

---

## 3. Leave Me Alone
- **Founder:** Danielle & James
- **Revenue:** $30k+ MRR
- **Category:** Productivity / Email
- **Description:** A service that allows users to easily unsubscribe from unwanted emails and newsletters with a single click, respecting privacy (they don't sell data).
- **Why It Works:** Solves a universal pain point (overloaded inbox). They differentiated from Unroll.me by focusing entirely on privacy and charging for the service (paid model vs free model that sells data).
- **Tech Stack:** Node.js, React, PostgreSQL
- **Source:** Starter Story Interview
